Rivet & Rail FAQ

Everything you need to know about your industrial-luxe stay.

01

What are the check-in and check-out times at Rivet & Rail?

+

Check-in begins at 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 12:00 PM. We offer luggage storage for guests arriving early or staying late to explore the city.

02

Is breakfast included in my reservation?

+

Yes, all guests at Rivet & Rail enjoy a complimentary artisanal breakfast served in the main lobby gallery from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM.

03

What is the Rivet & Rail cancellation policy?

+

Reservations can be cancelled free of charge up to 48 hours before your arrival date. Cancellations within the 48-hour window will be charged for the first night.

04

Is there on-site parking available?

+

We provide secure, 24/7 valet parking for our guests. There is a designated area for electric vehicle charging as well.
